Freight Wagon Available

Covered Box Wagons (CBX)

CBX wagons are used to transport a wide variety of goods both in bulk and pre-packed form such as:

  1. Grains (Maize, sorghum, soya, soya feed, others)
  2. Flour
  3. Fertilizer
  4. FMCG goods (biscuits, grooming products amongst others)
Minimum Tonnage per haulage: 600 MT | Maximum Tonnage per haulage: 1000 MT
Covered Goods Wagons (CGX)

CGX wagons are used to transport a wide variety of goods mostly in packed form loaded in pallets into containers. Such goods include fast moving consumer goods such as biscuits, drinks (cans, bottles), toiletries etc. Bagged cement can also be loaded on the flatbeds directly. Half-sided metal frames attached to the external sides of the wagon enable sheets or tarpaulin covers to be fastened to prevent the freight from getting wet in the case of direct loading.

Minimum Tonnage per haulage: 500 MT | Maximum Tonnage per haulage: 750 MTT
