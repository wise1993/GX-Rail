About us

GX Rail Commodities is a niche logistics service provider which seeks to leverage on the capacity of the recently modernised Nigerian Railway Corporation (NRC) to resolve the problems faced by manufacturers in the country with regard to logistics. We aim to provide a lasting solution, having obtained a clear understanding of the challenges our clients face in sourcing feedstock for end-user production/consumption in the face of exceptionally poor road networks that result in delays, accidents, and higher operating costs.

GX Rail Commodities thus offers an invaluable and seamless package by providing pick-up and delivery logistics services for manufacturers in the food/agro-allied and construction sector. Additionally, as a result of a strategic alliance with the NRC, GX Rail Commodities has a clear advantage over competitors, proven by its recognition for top quality service delivery

As a leading indigenous rail freight haulage and logistics provider, GX Rail Commodities offers a comprehensive portfolio of punctual, reliable, safe, and eco-friendly rail freight services. We believe rail haulage is the most effective and efficient solution to the significant mobility challenges faced by multinationals and SME’s in the Nigerian manufacturing sector.

Mission

GX Rail commodities is a rail services operator with a clear aim of reducing the present cost of logistics/ haulage in Nigeria by making rail haulage more attractive, accessible and cost effective to manufacturers/SME’s in Nigeria.

Vision

Our vision is to be recognised for our pride and professionalism in delivering innovative customer solutions and service excellence via our intermodal method of transportation. We also aim to be the 1st privately owned railway operators in Nigeria by 2018. Offering innovative solutions to logistics challenges faced by manufacturers and SME’s in Nigeria.
