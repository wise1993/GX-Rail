Rail Haulage

We provide rail haulage services for manufacturers with bulk commodities willing to move in a single shipment. We presently provide weekly services hauling commodities via the railway between the following locations:
- Lagos > Ijoko > Ibadan > Oshogbo > Ilorin > Minna > Kaduna > Zaria > Kano > Nguru
- Kano > Zaria > Kaduna > Minna > Ilorin > Oshogbo > Ibadan > joko > Lagos
Our aim is to have a rail freight available within close proximity to 75% of Nigeria’s industrial hubs and key consumer markets by December 2017
