High Capacity and Great Flexibility

We have the capacity to move up to 1000MT in a single shipment, efficiently and in a timely manner any day of the week. No broken down trucks, accidents or ineffective drivers. We deliver as promised.

Cost Effective Freight Transport

By selecting the right freight wagon for your needs and making optimal use of the available capacity and wagon loading limits, we ensure the most cost-effective means of transporting your goods. Also, we’ll not be beaten on prices when compared with road haulage.

Off Dock Warehousing Solutions for Eligible Products (Short & Long term)

We don’t just deliver your goods as promised, we can also provide warehousing facilities for both short and long term leasing in industrial hubs across regions we operate in.

Goods In Transit Insurance
